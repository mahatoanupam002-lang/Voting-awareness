SwarmAudit is a proposed civic-tech platform under the CJP open-source framework. All reports on this demo page are mock data for illustrative purposes. No real reports have been submitted. In production, each report would require GPS metadata, a photograph hash, and voluntary identity verification. Data is shared monthly with relevant municipal authorities under the provisions of the Right to Information Act, 2005. This page does not constitute legal advice.
