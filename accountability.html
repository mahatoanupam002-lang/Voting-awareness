Category I

Agriculture & Farmers

Pledges in this category: 3
Direct Paddy Procurement at MSP

BJP promised to procure paddy directly from farmers at the Central MSP (₹2,183/quintal for common paddy, FY26) through government agencies, eliminating middlemen and the APMC commission agent layer that depressed farmer receipts under TMC.

Source: BJP 2026 Manifesto (Agriculture chapter) · Suvendu Adhikari, rally speech, Paschim Medinipur, March 2026

No government order issued yet.

Source: BJP 2026 Manifesto (Welfare chapter) · Campaign literature, Purba Medinipur, February 2026

BJP pledged to expand the existing Swastha Saathi health insurance scheme (inherited from TMC) and increase the annual coverage cap from ₹5 lakh to ₹10 lakh per family, while expanding the empanelled hospital network to include private hospitals in district towns.

📋 How This Tracker Works

Each pledge is sourced from documented BJP statements (manifesto, press conferences, rally speeches). Status changes only when official government orders, GO numbers, or independently verified action is reported by credible news sources. Campaign rhetoric is not treated as delivery. This tracker is updated as events occur — check back regularly.

Status Definitions

Watching — Promise made; no government action yet. Default state until an order is issued.

Announced — Government has formally announced the policy/scheme via official communication.

In Progress — Implementation has physically started (tenders issued, beneficiary lists activated, etc.).

Delivered — Independent verification confirms the promise has been substantially fulfilled.

Delayed — Past the stated or implied deadline without completion.

Broken — Government has explicitly reversed the policy or confirmed it will not be implemented.

Sources: BJP 2026 West Bengal Election Manifesto · Campaign speeches (documented) · Press Conference transcripts · ADR India · Election Commission of India
