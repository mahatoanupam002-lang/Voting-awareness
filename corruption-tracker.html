How to Read This Dossier

Status definitions: Investigation — probe ongoing, no chargesheet yet. Trial — chargesheet filed, court hearings underway. Convicted — court has recorded a guilty verdict. Court Monitoring — Supreme Court or High Court directly supervising the case. Stalled — no significant progress for 12+ months.

Agency abbreviations: CBI — Central Bureau of Investigation. ED — Enforcement Directorate (financial crimes, PMLA). SC — Supreme Court of India. SFIO — Serious Fraud Investigation Office. NHRC — National Human Rights Commission.

Accused status: Arrested = taken into custody at some point. Bail = released on bail (case ongoing). Trial = charges framed, evidence being heard. Convicted = guilty verdict recorded. Absconding = evading arrest, look-out notice issued. Named = mentioned in FIR/chargesheet, not yet arrested.

Sources: CBI chargesheets (public domain), ED press releases, Supreme Court and Calcutta HC orders, SEBI adjudication orders, NHRC reports, and reporting by The Hindu, Indian Express, Anandabazar Patrika, and Hindustan Times. Last verified: May 2026.
